Summary
  • Burst is a 1st person puzzle game. As a construction worker in the future, you must use the original beam spawning game mechanic to create bridges, to block lasers, to hide from enemies, and much more! Burst was created in the Fall of 2014 as the final project for Computer Game Design (EECS 494) at University of Michigan. The members of the team are Jonah Scheinerman, Yu-Chen Lee, and Sean Goodrich.
Instructions
  • WASD Keys - Move
  • Space Bar - Jump
  • Mouse - Aim
  • Left Click - Shoot / Return Bursts
  • ESC button - Menu
Notes
  • To begin the game, walk to the back of the room and enter the door with the sign above that says "start game"
  • Return Bursts whenever possible as ammunition is limited
  • When the crosshair is blue, bursts can be spawned
  • When the crosshair is empty, the burst target is out of range
  • When the crosshair is red, bursts cannot be spawned. Either the user has run out of ammunition or the target material prevents bursts from being spawned
